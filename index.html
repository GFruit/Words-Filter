Words Filter

Wordset
Examples: Top 200 = top 200 most common english words; Quotes = 5000 quotes pulled from Typeracer


Hand/s
Examples: Both = Any word; Left = Only left-hand words; Right = Only right-hand words


Home Position
Examples: Off = Any word; On: Only home-position words


Length
Examples: Min-length 5 / Max-length 7 = Only words/quotes with length of at least 5 and at most 7


Excluding Wordparts (n-grams)
Example: he he, st, en in on = If the word contains 2x "he" or an "st" or an "en" and "in" and "on" it's excluded.


Including Wordparts (n-grams)
Example: he he, st, en in on = If the word contains 2x "he" or an "st" or an "en" and "in" and "on" it's included.



For slightly more detailled explanations visit: the GitHub's README file.

Layout
Example: Qwerty = Standard US Keyboard Layout


Rows
Examples: Top and Home on = Only include words that can be typed with the top and home row alone.


Fingers
Example: lp and rp off = Only include words that don't contain letters that are typed with the right and left pinky. (lp = left pinky | lr = left ring | lm = left middle | li = left index (and same for the right hand))


Excluding Letters
Example: a, m, st, io, eee = If the word contains an "a" or an "m" or an "s" and "t" or an "i" and "o" or 3x "e" it's excluded.


Including Letters
Example: a, m, st, io, eee = If the word contains an "a" or an "m" or an "s" and "t" or an "i" and "o" or 3x "e" it's included.


Alternate
Example: Hands and Fingers on = Only include words in which the hands and fingers alternate after each letter.
